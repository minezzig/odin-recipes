Creamy Vegan Mac and Cheese for Weekday Nights

This is an easy recipe you can make any day of the week. You just need 500g of your fave pasta, nutritional yeast, flour, non-dairy milk, non-dairy butter, and your favorite spices!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • butter
  • soy milk
  • nutritional yeast
  • pasta

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Melt butter
  3. add flour
  4. add soy milk
  5. whisk vigorously
  6. mix in nutritional yeast and spices
  7. pour pasta into sauce and mix thoroughly
